Lord's Waffles

Description

Lord and Savior Jesus APPROVES this tasty and crispy waffle, how can u not approve it when Jesus himself says that this is how every believer need to start his morning.

Ingredient

For 5 Servings cause you better have 3 kids

Cause Jesus Lovse kids

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 legs, i mean 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and half cups of warm Milk
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 third cup butter,melted, like your heart when you see JESUS
  • 2 tablesspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

If you got all those Ingrediants make sure to thank Jesus for it.

Steps

First of give yourself to the Lord and Savior, then follow the next

  1. Step 1

    In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature

  2. Step 2

    In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla.Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.

  3. last step to heaven

    Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
